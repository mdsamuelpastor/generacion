Before starting-up the gameserver you have to unzip the npc_drops and geo_data-files:

- Unzip the content from the npc_drops.7z (*repo*/trunk/AL-Game/xml/) to *compiled_server*/data/static_data/npc_drops/
- Unzip the content from geo.7z (*repo*/trunk/AL-Game/geo_files/) to *compiled_server*/data/geo/


If you have deactivated FFA in custom.properties (gameserver.ffa.enable = false), unzip the content from the no_FFA.7z (*repo*/trunk/AL-Game/xml/) to *compiled_server*/data/


To uncompress the files you need 7-zip (http://www.7-zip.org/download.html)
